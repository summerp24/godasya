How We Make It

From Udder to Jar – Pure & Simple

No shortcuts. No factories. Our 5-step traditional process takes 3 days — because quality cannot be rushed.

🐄

Gir Cow Milking

Fresh A2 milk collected ethically from our Gir cows every morning after the calf has been fed.

🥛

Curd Setting

Milk is naturally curdled overnight using traditional starter cultures — no lab shortcuts.

🔄

Bilona Churning

Curd is slowly hand-churned in the traditional Bilona method to separate pure white butter.

🔥

Slow Wood-Fire Simmering

Butter is simmered on a low wood fire to evaporate all moisture — creating pure, golden ghee.

📦

Hygienic Bottling

Filtered and poured into food-grade glass jars, sealed and labelled — ready for your family.

Our Story

A Family's Promise of Purity

Godasya was born from a simple belief — every Indian family deserves ghee that is truly pure, the way it was made for generations before us. The name 'Godasya' (गोदास्य) is rooted in Sanskrit, meaning "one who serves the cow" — reflecting our deep respect for Gir cows and the gift of A2 milk.

We are a small family operation based in Maharashtra. Our Gir cows are raised with love, fed naturally, and never injected with hormones. The entire process — from milking to churning to packaging — happens within our family farm under our watchful eyes.
